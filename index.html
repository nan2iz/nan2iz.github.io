SpeechBuddy

The application provides English training lessons that based on users' English requirement and conducts an algorithm to evaluate the speaking ability and how well of a pronunciation of the learners by applying Automatic Speech Recognition techniques and Natural Language Process. The application helps English learners improve their speaking skill without the requirement to communicate with native speakers. With this application, English learners can practice speaking with a machine and the application will collect and show the results and recommend the way to improve their speaking skill based on their result. Thus, English learners can learn English from their home with the application that customize learning resources for the learners. In conclusion, the SpeechBuddy not only enhanced the students' English Speaking but also encouraged students to be able to practice English without faintheartedness and developed more positive attitudes about English learning.

Features

  • Listen how to pronouce with SpeechBuddy
  • Repeat after SpeechBuddy
  • SpeechBuddy analyze your pronouciation
  • You can customize your lessons too
  • SpeechBuddy listens to you and provide feedbacks
  • Keep tracking your errors

Tools

Server: Python & Django, NLTK, gTTS, Google Speech API

Client: HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Web Speech API
